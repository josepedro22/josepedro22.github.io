About Us

Founded in 2020, Doçaria da Manu was born from the dream of a married woman and mother of two, who have been her greatest helpers and inspirations from the very beginning. Located in the charming city of Guimarães, Doçaria da Manu specializes in crafting sweets that are not only delicious but also true works of personalized art.
